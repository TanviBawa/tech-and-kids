Balancing Technology and Hands-On Learning

Ensuring children benefit from both digital tools and real-world experiences.

Family Involvement and Hands-On Learning

Families play a vital role in balancing technology use in early childhood education. Encouraging children to explore outside of screens, participate in creative activities, and engage in social play helps maintain healthy development.

Teachers can involve families by sharing learning resources, suggesting home activities, and organizing workshops to demonstrate effective ways to integrate technology without overuse.
